About AllRecipes

AllRecipes is a trusted community of home cooks, culinary experts, and food enthusiasts who come together to share, create, and explore the world of cooking. Since its inception, AllRecipes has become a go-to resource for millions of people seeking inspiration, guidance, and recipes that are easy to make and delicious.

Our Mission

At AllRecipes, our mission is to empower people to cook confidently and enjoy the process of making food. We believe that everyone can be a great cook with the right support, which is why we provide reliable recipes, step-by-step instructions, and helpful tips that make cooking accessible to everyone.

Our Community

With over 100K+ recipes and contributions from people all over the world, our community is at the heart of everything we do. From classic family recipes to innovative new dishes, the diversity of our recipe collection reflects the creativity and passion of our users.

Quality You Can Trust

We understand that quality matters. That’s why every recipe on AllRecipes is rigorously tested and reviewed by our team to ensure that you can trust the recipes to work every time. Our reviews and ratings from real users offer additional insights to help you make informed decisions in the kitchen.

Our Features

Beyond recipes, AllRecipes offers a range of features to support your culinary journey:

  • Step-by-step cooking videos and guides
  • Personalized recipe recommendations
  • Meal planning tools and shopping lists
  • In-depth articles on cooking techniques, ingredient spotlights, and seasonal guides

Our Promise

We are dedicated to creating a welcoming space where everyone can share their love for cooking. We promise to continue providing reliable recipes, support, and inspiration to make every cooking experience enjoyable and successful.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, we’d love to hear from you. Reach out to us through our Contact Us page.

Join Us!

Join the AllRecipes community today and start sharing your recipes, reviewing dishes, and discovering new culinary inspiration. Together, we’re making cooking a joyful and rewarding experience for everyone.
