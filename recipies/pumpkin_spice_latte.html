Pumpkin Spice Latte
⭐ 4.2 (70 Reviews)
Submitted by SLEEEPY | Updated on July 14, 2022
Jump to Ingredients | Jump to Directions | Jump to Nutritional Values
Prep Time: 2 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 7 mins
My take on our corner coffee shop's seasonal pumpkin latte. I loved it so much I HAD to do something! You can use canned 'pumpkin pie mix' if you like, since it contains the pumpkin pie spice ingredients already. Top with nutmeg or cinnamon if you like. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger brewed espresso

Directions

Step 1:

Brew your espresso. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, whisk together the pumpkin, vanilla, sugar, pumpkin pie spice and milk. Warm over medium heat, whisking constantly, until hot and frothy. Do not bring to a boil.

Step 2:

Pour the espresso into a mug and pour the pumpkin spiced milk over it.
